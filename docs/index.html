Glåüm Philosophy

What is Glåüm Consciousness

Glåüm consciousness refers to the state of awareness and perception achieved by individuals who actively embrace and embody the principles of compassion, unity, and empathetic understanding as central tenets of their existence. It signifies a heightened sense of interconnectedness with others, a deep recognition of the inherent value and dignity of every being, and a commitment to fostering harmony and empathy within the Glåüm community.

About

From Satire to Community

Glåüm had humble beginnings as a cult satire, which originated from the whimsical idea of creating a fictional organization that hilariously claimed to be the ultimate solution to all problems. Through printed pamphlets and playful messaging, we sought to poke fun at the concept of self-proclaimed enlightenment and exaggerated the outrageous benefits of Glåüm.

However, as Glåüm evolved, something unexpected happened. The satirical nature of Glåüm struck a chord with many individuals who were seeking connection and a sense of belonging in a world that sometimes feels disconnected. The community that emerged around Glåüm became very real, fostering authentic relationships and connections.

We acknowledge and appreciate the whimsy and silliness that defined Glåüm's early years. At the same time, we understand the importance of addressing the term "cult" and being transparent about our values, so that Glåüm can feel accessible and approachable to all.



Defining "Cult" in the Context of Glåüm

The term "cult" is often loosely defined and carries negative connotations. To provide more clarity, we would like to redefine "cult" in the context of Glåüm, emphasizing our core values of culture, cultivation, and transparency.

Culture: Glåüm embraces a culture where diversity of thought and perspectives are not only respected but encouraged. We value open and respectful discussions, welcoming viewpoints from all walks of life. Our goal is to foster an inclusive and accepting community.

Cultivation: Glåüm is committed to personal and collective growth. We believe in the power of self-reflection, consciousness-raising practices, and mutual support in our journey towards higher states of awareness. Our focus is on nurturing individual and collective well-being.

Transparency: We believe in being open, honest, and transparent in our actions and communications. Glåüm strives to provide clarity regarding our mission, values, and activities. We encourage our community to ask questions, seek understanding, and engage in meaningful dialogue.

By redefining "cult" through the lens of culture, cultivation, and transparency, we aim to cultivate an environment where everyone feels welcome, respected, and free to explore their own understanding of Glåüm.
