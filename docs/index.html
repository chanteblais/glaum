What is Glåüm?

Glåüm is a cult.
But not the kind you were warned about.

We call ourselves Creatures Undergoing Loving Transformation—sometimes Transcendence, depending on the weather. Our compass points to three things: compassion, connection, and playfulness—in that order.

Compassion is the root. Without it, the rest collapses.
Connection is the trunk, holding us upright together.
Playfulness is the fruit—bright, unpredictable, and sometimes suspiciously purple.

⸻

Glåüm exists to bring people together through improbable projects, loving gatherings, and shared acts of creation. We offer space for art to grow, for ideas to be tested, for healing to take shape in strange and tender ways.

Yes, there are rituals. Yes, there are symbols whose meanings are not immediately clear. This is part of the attunement process.

Those who join the Manyhands often report side effects:
• spontaneous teamwork with strangers
• sudden clarity of purpose
• an irresistible urge to adorn your hands with smaller hands.

We are guided by strong principles, but not by rigid rules.
We are playful, but not careless.
We are serious about love.
